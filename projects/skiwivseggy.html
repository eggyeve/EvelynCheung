Skiwi VS Eggy

Skiwi vs Eggy (2025 June - Present)

A graduate short film directed by Evelyn Cheung and Bonnie Wright

We have been coming up with where ourselves are turned to the main characters of the film, we have always wanted to work on a film involving hyperactive actions scenes and Visual Effects, also exploring mixed media (2D & 3D)

Story

Two rival flightless bird species, kiwis and penguins, clash when a kiwi pilot crash-lands on a sun-scorched island Mistaken intentions spark a chaotic showdown, turning survival into all-out war
