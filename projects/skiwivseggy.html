Skiwi VS Eggy

Skiwi vs Eggy (2025 June - Present)

A graduate short film directed by Evelyn Cheung and Bonnie Wright

The project explores high energy action sequences and blends 2D and 3D animation techniques.

Our aim was to push creative boundaries through strong storytelling and visual Effects

Story Summary

Two rival flightless bird species, kiwis and penguins, clash when a kiwi pilot crash lands on a sun scorched island Mistaken intentions spark a chaotic showdown, turning survival into an all out war
